About NTURO

NTURO is a race for all multi-sports enthusiast to have a platform for competitive race for both the swim and run discipline. This race is a non-stop burst of swim-run-swim-run-swim-run without any breaks. With the added element of speed of transition during the race.

Participants will then not only enjoy the race but also to have exposure for newcomers venturing into this field of sport. This can be regarded as one of the platforms that will push the participants beyond their limits to see how far they can go.

Race Information

Date 16th March 2019
Time 0700hrs - 1500hrs
Location NTU Sports and Recreation Center
Race Format 3 sets of ( 600m swim and 2km run )

Swim Map

The swim will commence first with 2 swimmers at the intervals of 10 seconds per wave completing a total of 300m which is 6 laps of 50m. For the first 300m swim, all participants are to start at the lane 1 and lane 2 position. Only after they are done with their first 2km run, they will enter from lane 3 to complete their second 300m swim and lastly the last set at lane 6. In total, every participant will complete a total of 900m swim and they will collect a coloured tag rubber band at the exit point after completing their swim.

Run Map

Nanyang Green > Lien Ying Chow Dr > Nanyang Cres > Carpark Entrance > Nanyang Green. This is one loop of 2km, all participants are to complete 3 loops and to end their run at the finishing point.
